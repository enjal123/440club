About Salon Tuntun

Salon Tuntun is more than just a place for a good time—it’s an experience. Nestled in the heart of Oaxaca, we offer a fusion of culture, music, and unforgettable flavors. Whether you're here for a cultural event, a lively celebration, or simply to enjoy good food and drinks, we are proud to be a part of the community and to offer an atmosphere that welcomes everyone. Our mission is to showcase the best of Oaxaca, honoring its traditions while bringing modern flare to every event.
